# VOICEOVER

---SCENE:1---
Have you ever looked up at the sky and wondered why it's blue? It turns out the answer is more fascinating than you might think.
---END---
---SCENE:2---
Sunlight contains all colors of the rainbow. When it hits Earth's atmosphere, the light scatters off tiny molecules. Shorter wavelengths like blue scatter way more than red. That scattered blue light comes at you from every direction, making the entire sky appear blue.
---END---
---SCENE:3---
And that's also why sunsets are red. The light travels through more atmosphere, so most blue gets scattered away before it reaches your eyes. Nature's physics, happening right above your head every single day.
---END---
